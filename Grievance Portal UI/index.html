Welcome to the Grievance Portal !


A grievance and redressal portal serves as a digital platform designed to address and resolve issues, complaints, or grievances raised by individuals or entities. It provides a structured mechanism for users to submit their concerns, feedback, or complaints regarding various matters, such as services, policies, or misconduct. These portals typically offer a user-friendly interface where users can easily register their grievances by filling out predefined forms or providing relevant details. Once a grievance is submitted, it undergoes a systematic process of review and resolution by designated authorities or administrators.
