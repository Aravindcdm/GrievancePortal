Our Mission

We are committed to addressing grievances promptly and impartially, while upholding the values of integrity and respect.

- An easy-to-use platform for submitting grievances online. - Transparent and anonymous viewing of grievances by the management team. - Timely updates on the status and resolution of grievances. - A feedback mechanism to help us improve our grievance redressal process.

Users can submit their grievances through the portal, which will then be reviewed and addressed by the relevant authorities. The management team will oversee the process to ensure that grievances are handled in a timely and effective manner.
